Rohan was a kind and hardworking college student. Every day, he traveled to college by bus and worked part-time in the evening to support his studies. One rainy morning, while walking to the bus stop, he noticed a black wallet lying near the roadside. He picked it up and looked inside. The wallet contained some cash, several cards, and an identification card. The owner's name was Mr. Sharma, a retired school teacher who lived nearby. Rohan knew that losing a wallet could cause a lot of stress and trouble. For a moment, he thought about how useful the money could be for him. His college fees were due, and he was facing financial difficulties. However, he quickly pushed that thought aside. He believed that honesty was more valuable than money. After reaching college, Rohan searched for Mr. Sharma's contact information online. Fortunately, he found a phone number linked to the address on the identification card. He called the number and explained that he had found the wallet. Mr. Sharma was surprised and relieved. He had been searching for the wallet all morning and was worried because it contained important documents. They agreed to meet that evening at a nearby park. When Rohan arrived at the park, Mr. Sharma was waiting anxiously. As soon as he saw the wallet, a smile spread across his face. He checked the contents and found that everything was exactly as he had left it. Mr. Sharma thanked Rohan warmly and offered him a reward. However, Rohan politely refused. He said that he had only done what was right and did not expect anything in return. Touched by Rohan's honesty, Mr. Sharma asked about his studies and future goals. Rohan shared his dream of becoming a software engineer but also mentioned the financial challenges he faced. A few days later, Mr. Sharma visited Rohan's college. He spoke with the principal and learned more about Rohan's excellent academic performance. Impressed by his character and dedication, Mr. Sharma decided to help. He arranged a scholarship through a local educational trust to support Rohan's education. The scholarship covered a significant portion of Rohan's expenses and allowed him to focus more on his studies. With renewed confidence, he worked hard and eventually graduated with excellent results. Years later, Rohan became a successful software engineer. He never forgot the lesson that honesty had taught him. Whenever he had the opportunity, he helped students who faced financial difficulties, just as Mr. Sharma had helped him. The incident of the lost wallet became a turning point in his life. It showed that doing the right thing may not bring immediate rewards, but good deeds often return in unexpected ways. Rohan's honesty not only helped a stranger recover his belongings but also opened the door to a brighter future for himself.
